Cockroaches

Cockroaches are highly adaptable insects that can invade homes through various means, such as cracks, plumbing, and shared walls.

How They Invade Homes

  • Through cracks and gaps around doors, windows, and foundations.
  • Via plumbing and sewer systems, emerging from drains and pipes.
  • Hitching a ride in grocery bags, boxes, and other containers.
  • Moving between units in multi-unit buildings through shared walls.
  • Entering from outdoor spaces, especially gardens, in search of food, water, and shelter.

How to Prevent Infestations

  • Seal entry points by closing off cracks and gaps.
  • Maintain cleanliness in kitchens and dining areas.
  • Eliminate moisture by fixing leaks and using dehumidifiers.
  • Properly manage waste by using sealed trash cans.
  • Inspect packages for cockroaches before bringing them inside.
  • Use baits and traps in areas where cockroaches are likely to travel.
